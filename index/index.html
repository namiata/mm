About

We are James Madison University's drivers club. Our goal is to help students enter the world of Motorsports. We help students take their first steps into auto-cross, drag racing, karting, road-course racing, competition showing, and drifting, among other types of racing. We do not condone street racing or other illegal activities, and instead, we offer a safe and regulated space for racing. Our main focus is Motorsports, but we are also a social club welcoming all people who like cars, bikes, trucks, or anything with an engine. Anyone with a passion for automobiles is welcome to join as we are a great place to meet and befriend other like-minded people and make connections in the automotive world.
